How To Play

You are given three words each day, all encrypted using the Caesar Cipher from 1-10.

The Caesar Cipher encrypts a letter by a certain amount: 'A' with a cipher of three becomes 'D' for example.

Your job is to decrypt each word, meaning: Figure out the cipher for each word, and subtract that amount from each letter.

You are given three guesses per word, the closer the guess, the more blue the word becomes.

Good luck!
